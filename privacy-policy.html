Overview

Google Classroom Assignment Tracker ("the Product") is a Google Sheets-based tool that helps teachers view and manage assignments from Google Classroom in a unified dashboard. This privacy policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and how we protect your information.

Information We Collect

Data Accessed from Google Classroom API

When you use this product, we request permission to access the following information from your Google Classroom account:

Courses Information:

  • Course names
  • Course IDs
  • Course enrollment status (active/archived)

Assignment Information:

  • Assignment titles
  • Due dates
  • Assignment types (assignment, quiz, test, etc.)
  • Point values
  • Assignment descriptions

Student Information:

  • Student names (first and last)
  • Student IDs (Google's internal identifier, not personally identifiable)
  • Enrollment in your courses

Submission Information:

  • Whether a student has submitted an assignment
  • Submission status (turned in, missing, late, returned)
  • Submission timestamps

Data We Do NOT Use or Display

Important Disclosure:
While our requested API scopes technically grant access to grade data (required by Google Classroom API structure), we do NOT use, display, or store:

  • Student grades or scores (our code ignores the assignedGrade field)
  • Assignment content or student work submissions
  • Student personal email addresses (beyond basic profile data)
  • Private messages or announcements
  • Guardian information
  • Any data from courses where you are not enrolled as a teacher

What We Actually Access:
Our code only reads submission status (submitted, missing, late, returned) and submission timestamps. Grade values are available in the API response but are NOT accessed, displayed, or stored by our code.

How We Use Your Information

Storage Location

ALL data imported from Google Classroom is stored exclusively in YOUR Google Drive in the Google Sheet you create. We do NOT:

  • Store data on external servers
  • Send data to third-party services
  • Maintain copies of your data
  • Access your data after initial import

Purpose of Data Access

The data is used solely to:

  1. Display assignment information in your private dashboard
  2. Generate missing work reports for your use
  3. Create student roster summaries
  4. Provide you with organizational tools for classroom management

Who Can See Your Data

  • Only YOU can see the data in your Google Sheet (unless you explicitly share the sheet)
  • We (the product creator) CANNOT see your data
  • The data remains private within your Google account
  • You control all sharing permissions via Google Drive settings

Data Retention and Deletion

How Long Data is Stored

  • Data remains in YOUR Google Sheet until you delete it
  • You can refresh/re-import data at any time, which overwrites old data
  • You control retention periods completely

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Delete the Google Sheet: Go to Google Drive → Right-click the sheet → "Remove"
  2. Revoke App Permissions: Go to Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access → Remove "Google Classroom Assignment Tracker"
  3. Clear Data: Use the "Clear All Data" button in the Settings tab (if you want to keep the sheet but clear data)

Data Retention After Deletion

Once you delete the Google Sheet or revoke permissions:

  • We retain NO copies of your data
  • All data is permanently deleted from your Google Drive
  • API access is immediately revoked

Data Security

How We Protect Your Data

OAuth 2.0 Authentication:

  • Secure, industry-standard authentication
  • You authorize access via Google's secure consent screen
  • Access can be revoked at any time

Read-Only Access:

  • The Product uses READ-ONLY scopes for Google Classroom API
  • We CANNOT modify, delete, or post to your Google Classroom
  • We CANNOT change grades or assignment settings

No External Storage:

  • Data never leaves Google's infrastructure
  • No external databases or servers
  • All processing happens within Google Apps Script (Google's platform)

Compliance with Educational Privacy Laws

FERPA Compliance (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act)

This product is designed to comply with FERPA requirements:

  1. Teacher as "School Official": Teachers using this product are acting as school officials with legitimate educational interest in student data
  2. No Unauthorized Disclosure: Student data is NOT shared with third parties
  3. Student Records Protection: Data remains within the teacher's secure Google account
  4. Parental Rights: Schools and teachers remain responsible for FERPA compliance and parental notification

Important: If your school or district has specific policies about third-party tools or data processing agreements (DPAs), please consult with your IT administrator or privacy officer before using this product.

COPPA Compliance (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

This product:

  • Does NOT collect personal information directly from students
  • Accesses only the information teachers already have via Google Classroom
  • Does NOT market to children
  • Requires teacher (adult) authorization for all data access

  • Google Classroom API: For accessing classroom data
  • Google Sheets: For storing and displaying data
  • Google Drive: For file storage

All Google services are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

No Other Third Parties

  • External databases
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Marketing automation tools

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View: All data is visible in your Google Sheet
  • Edit: You can manually edit any cell in the sheet
  • Delete: Delete the sheet or revoke access at any time
  • Export: Use Google Sheets' export features (File → Download)

This product's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
